[jnAnayOga]
 

 
The technique of realizing the Self by contemplation.
 

When someone explains a concept to us, we could get a deeper understanding by giving it deep thought, avoiding distractions, meditating and seeking perfection. This is contemplation. Realizing the true nature of the Self via contemplation is [jnAnayOga]. It includes the realization of the relationship between the Self and the Lord.

As Krishna says in the Gita, our body is designed for constant change and activity. It's almost impossible to focus in this way and exclude everything else. Any distraction will set you back, making it more difficult to get back on track.

That's why He says that the preferred method to realization is through activity rather than contemplation.
